About Us



Oxygen Media Plus Music Group (OMPMG) - OMPMG is a full service media company that primarily provides multimedia content, online music distribution, mass communication, managing of events, promotions, photography, graphic and video editing with blue screen facilities, complete music production, voice-overs, advertisement solutions, consultants and other services. OMPMG staff is well experience and continually trained to meet new technologies and growth. One of our core values is to improve the services that we provide to the entertainment industry and its related fields.

OMPMG utilizes renowned technology and is solution oriented in its approach with a global awareness. Our services provide an affordable alternative for our partners and stakeholders. OMPMG is family oriented in its approach and strongly believes in love, dedication, a solid business model with a core staff to be successful. This company provides excellent customer service. Our human resource is the heart of our strength.

OMPMG has worked with renowned record labels, highly achieved entertainers, producers and promoters on a host of projects. We serve by the motto “breathe freely” and we aim to continue providing fast, efficient, affordable, high quality services to our clients in the entertainment industry. We believe that today’s vision is tomorrow’s reality as we aspire to make all the dreams and visions of our clients into a possibility. Oxygen Media Plus Music Group, a fresh approach.
